Who we are

Built by editors and artists, not account managers

3TAD Studio is an independent video editing and 3D animation house. We work directly with the people who shoot, direct, and brief — no layers, no hand-offs, no diluted feedback.

Every project moves through the same two disciplines: story-driven editing and dimensional 3D work, brought together under one roof so the look stays consistent from first cut to final render.
